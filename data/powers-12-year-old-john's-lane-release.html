An incredibly special release that’s won practically every Whiskey award going.

Formerly a wool mill, the Midleton Distillery, named after Lord Midleton who inherited the building, subsequently turning it into a what is now one of Ireland’s most iconic and loved distilleries from a mill in 1825 with his two brothers. The distillery includes the World’s largest still with a capacity of over 31,000 gallons!

Powers John Lane is consistently described as one of the best Irish Whiskeys created. It typifies Irish Whiskey in a truly classic way. Mixing maturation in Bourbon and Oloroso Sherry casks, this Whiskey is all about balance in ways rarely seen in the category.
