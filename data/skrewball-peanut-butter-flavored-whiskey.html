It’s exactly what you expect — a peanut-infused Whiskey.
 
Skrewball Whiskey has one product. Well, one Whiskey anyway. But let’s come back to that. Our story starts with the realization that many southeast Asian dishes are made with peanuts or are served with peanut sauce. Well, there is a small restaurant in Ocean Beach, California — near San Diego — called OB Noodle House. The owner and bartender (one guy) mixed up his own Whiskey infusion cocktail to go with the food he was serving. And that brings us to today.
 
Skrewball Peanut Butter Whiskey is just what is sounds like — an American Whiskey infused with peanut butter. And we get it that this might sounds crazy at first, but think about it. Classic Whiskey flavors include vanilla, honey, toffee, grains, and stone fruits. In a way, it’s like having a PB&J sandwich in an adult beverage at 35% ABV.
 
Smartass corner:
OB Noodle House has been featured on several television shows, including Guy Fieri’s “Diners, Drive Ins, & Dives.”
